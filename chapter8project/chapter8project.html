Lawrence River Trail

Meandering along the Kaw River, the River Trail is a wooded ten-mile single-lane loop located in North Lawrence. It's rated easy, but there are plenty of tight turns, rocks, logs, and poisonous snakes to keep you on your toes.

Directions:

Take I-70 to Lawrence, follow signs to the river trail.

Trail Rules:

  1. Only ride when the trail is open.
  2. Riders go clockwise, hikers go counterclockwise.
  3. Stay on marked trails.
  4. Do not disturb wildlife.
  5. Don't feed the snakes.
